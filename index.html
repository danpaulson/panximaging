PanX Imaging: Bringing innovative high-definition, wide-field-of-view, ‘as your eye sees it’ digital imaging solutions to industries with unique and challenging needs. Industries with such needs include, but are not limited, to security and surveillance, sports photography, military, and consumer photography.

Our unique designs utilize multi-lens sensor arrays and a patented image integration (stitching) algorithm which;

  • Allows creation of extremely wide field of view virtual images (> 210o)
  • Minimizes and/or eliminates the need for gimbals to provide pan, zoom and tilt functionality
  • Captures images in dimly lit spaces while maintaining feature recognition and enhancement ability
  • Supports the ability to discern objects of interest in great detail for sub-mapping, analysis, mensuration
